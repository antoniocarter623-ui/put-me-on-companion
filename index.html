App Features

The Live Stage

The core of the classroom. When the Professor spins a track, it appears here. You grade it in real-time on 5 criteria: Flow, Production, Lyrics, Vibe, and Aesthetics.

GPA & Rank

Your musical taste defines your rank. Grade songs consistently to earn your GPA. Level up from Freshman to Senior, and unlock the "Label Exec" badge by finding Platinum tracks.

Vibe Match

Visit any student's profile to see your compatibility score. If you both follow each other, you unlock a "Vibe Connection" visual!

The Crate & Projects

Save your favorite tracks to "My Crate". Create custom playlists called "Projects" to share your curation skills with the class.
